Science Writing Editor

Better than Grammarly?

This project is meant to help you with your writing needs. Although grammarly is a great tool, there are some more light-hearted and specifialty tools that they don't have.

   

Pronoun Detector

   

This tool will highlight any personal pronouns that you use, specifically "we" and "I".

   

Wordcloud Generator

   

Want to be able to get the gist of an essay? Use this wordcloud generator to see what words show up the most!

   

Text Similarity Score

   

Curious how similar the vocabulary of two documents are? Put in the two writing samples and get back a similarity score.
